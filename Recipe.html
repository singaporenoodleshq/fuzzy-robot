    Method

    1. Soak rice noodles in warm water until soft.
    2. Heat oil in a wok and cook prawns until pink.
    3. Add garlic, onion, and curry powder.
    4. Stir in noodles, soy sauce, and vegetables.
    5. Toss everything together and serve hot.
